Zoo Aquarium de Madrid - Wikipedia

Zoo Aquarium de Madrid is a 20-hectare (49-acre) zoo and aquarium located in the Casa de Campo in Madrid, Spain. The zoo is home to over 6,000 animals of 500 different species, and is one of the few zoos in the world to house giant pandas.
